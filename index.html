BTEC IT

BTEC IT is a practical study program that focuses on real-world skills and applications in the field of Information Technology. It gives students the opportunity to learn various topics such as software development, web design, databases, networking, and cybersecurity. Unlike traditional education, BTEC IT is based on projects and practical assessments instead of just final exams, which helps students develop real skills needed in the job market. Many students prefer it because it combines theoretical learning with hands-on experience, and it opens many doors for further university study or careers in technology.
